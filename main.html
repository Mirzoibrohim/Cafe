About US!

Restaurant & Cafe

There is plenty of choice in food and beverages at City University Malaysia to keep you going throughout the day. Whether you fancy a hearty breakfast, a healthy, wholesome salad, a scrumptious slice of cake, or simply a hot chocolate drink, our great value menus have something for everyone and are available from 8am onwards on weekdays. Each member of our F&B staff is carefully trained on the skills and philosophies behind the highest standards of customer service in order to build a strong foundation for exceptional customer experiences. From new local and overseas friendships-for-life to easily-made professional contacts, our outlets can refresh all aspects of lives by offering space and opportunity for fun interaction among students, academicians, professionals, and visitors.
